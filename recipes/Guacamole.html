Guacamole for every day

No matter if for chips, tacos or for toast- Guacmole is always a tasty and easy treat.

And really easy to make as well! I will show you the steps.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt
  • Koriander
  • Tabasco

Step by Step Instructions

  1. First you take a ripe Avocado, cut it in half and remove the seed.
  2. Take a spoon and put all of the fruit in a bowl. Now mash it up with a fork.
  3. Put your lime juice, salt and tabasco in it and mix well.
  4. Shop a generous amount of Koriander and put it in as well. Now it is time to taste test
