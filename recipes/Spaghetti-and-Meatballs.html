Delicious Spaghetti and Meatballs

Quite nothing moves the heart as much as a hearty portion of Spaghetti and Meatballs

With this recipe you can recreate that taste of home and have an amazing meal

Ingredients

  • Minced Meat Pork and Beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Canned Tomatos
  • Olive Oil

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Make your Meatballs.
  2. Fry them in a pan in olive oil and add the whole can of tomatos
  3. Let it simmer and cook your Spaghetti
  4. Add them in your sauce pan and it is time to taste. Additionally you can grate some Parmesan.
