Classic and easy French Fries recipe

Description

In this recipe we will make crunchy and classic fast food french fries. Easy to make at home and sinfully delicious.

Ingredients

  • Rustic Potatos
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Step by Step

  1. For this easy recipe you need to peel and wash your rustic potatos.
  2. After that cut them into thin fries and soak them in water for 30 Minutes.
  3. Heat up your oil and fry them for 3 Minutes at 180 Grad Celsius.
  4. We will double fry them for extra cruch.
  5. Let your fries rest for 20 Minutes and fry theam again at 200 Grad Clesius for 2 Minutes.
  6. Put salt on them and en voila, time to eat!
